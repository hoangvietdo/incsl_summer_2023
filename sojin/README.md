## Task 3
$$\begin{aligned} x_r &= a \cos(bt)\\ 
                  \dot x_r &= - a \cdot b \sin (bt)\\
                  \ddot x_r &= - a \cdot b^2 \cos(bt)
  \end{aligned}$$

$$\begin{aligned} y_r &= a \sin(bt)\\ 
                  \dot y_r &= a \cdot b \cos (bt)\\
                  \ddot y_r &= - a \cdot b^2 \sin(bt)
  \end{aligned}$$
